Best Practices for Accurate Assessment
Do:
  • Provide detailed and honest answers
  • Include implementation details in comments
  • Consult with your development team
  • Review your security documentation
  • Consider all deployment environments
Don't:
  • Guess if you're uncertain about implementation
  • Overstate your security measures
  • Skip questions that seem irrelevant
  • Rush through the assessment
  • Ignore partial implementations
